What we do ?

We assist you with insurance claims for your policies purchased from Bank, Online, direct (Insurance company) or from any other agent. In simple terms we counsel, collect documents, check eligibility, review and prepare claim per admissibility, interact with the Third Party Administrators (TPA) and ensure fast and highest claim settlement*.

We support all type of claims across all insurance products. We support reimbursement and cashless claim in case of health insurance at network hospitals.

How we do ?

We start with online / offline counselling followed by the assessment of case along with documents. Review , tagging and processing the documents with insurance company / TPA and following up for claim settlement in accordance to your eligibility and claim admissibility. We also offer optional doorstep service.
