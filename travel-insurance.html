Travel Insurance

Travel Insurance covers your financial liability, if any, whenever you travel within or beyond the international waters of India.

The duration of the travel for one time can be 180 days at the maximum. The policyholder can take more than one trip in a year. Your Travel Insurance will cover:

  • Loss of Baggage
  • Loss of Passport
  • Hijacking
  • Medical Emergencies
  • Delayed Flights
  • Accidental Deaths

Important Note: Most policies come with a first USD 100 as deductible for each claim. Also please the cashless facility would be extended only for claims above USD 500.

Caution Note: Your claim will be as per your policy wordings from your insurance company. Information on this site is a general awareness information and not a legal document. Hence its important to read the complete policy wordings for full and correct understanding on the exact coverages.
