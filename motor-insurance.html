Motor Insurance

A Motor Insurance Policy is mandatory to be able to drive legally in India.

Broadly there are two types:

  • Third-Party Liability 
  • Comprehensive Package Policy.

A Third-Party Policy covers for all losses faced in a situation where your vehicle damages any third-party such as a public property, person or third-party vehicle. Third-Party Policy is the mandatory requirement to be able to drive legally in India, as stated by the Motor Vehicles Act.

A Comprehensive Package Policy covers both third-party damages and liabilities and damages caused to you and your own vehicle. The losses may arise due to an accident, theft, fire, natural calamities, and others.

Add-ons covers to your Comprehensive Package Policies for Cars and Bikes that act as additional shields to your vehicle, such as:

  • Tyre Protect Cover 
  • Zero Depreciation Cover 
  • Return to Invoice 
  • Engine and Gearbox Protection
  • Breakdown Assistance Cover

Important Note: Valid Insurance, DL and Registration is a must for all motor claims.

Caution Note: Your claim will be as per your policy wordings from your insurance company. Information on this site is a general awareness information and not a legal document. Hence, it’s important to read the complete policy wordings for full and correct understanding on the exact coverages.
